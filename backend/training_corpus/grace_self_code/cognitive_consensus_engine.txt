## consensus_engine.py
"\nMulti-Model Consensus Engine вЂ” Knights of the Roundtable\n\nA 4-layer deliberation protocol where multiple LLMs independently tackle the\nsame problem, then converge through consensus, alignment, and verification.\n\nLayer 1 вЂ” Independent Deliberation:\n  Each selected model receives the same prompt/problem/context independently.\n  No model sees another's output.\n\nLayer 2 вЂ” Consensus Formation:\n  All responses are collected, compared, and synthesised into a unified position.\n  Areas of agreement strengthen; disagreements are flagged for deeper analysis.\n\nLayer 3 вЂ” Alignment:\n  The consensus is aligned to Grace's internal world, user context, and the\n  specific problem domain. If Grace initiated the query autonomously, alignment\n  is to her own needs and the system's integrity.\n\nLayer 4 вЂ” Verification:\n  The aligned output passes through the existing verification pipeline:\n  trust scoring, hallucination guard, contradiction detection, and\n  deterministic checks.\n\nAutonomous Mode:\n  Grace can invoke the roundtable when she's stuck вЂ” needs deterministic\n  answers or faces ambiguity. Autonomous queries are batched to control\n  token costs (daily/weekly configurable).\n\nAvailable Models:\n  - Opus 4.6   (Anthropic Claude) вЂ” deep reasoning, audit, architecture\n  - Kimi 2.5   (Moonshot AI)      вЂ” long-context, document analysis\n  - Qwen 2.5   (via Ollama)       вЂ” local, fast, code-oriented\n  - Reasoning   (via Ollama)       вЂ” local reasoning model (configurable)\n"
